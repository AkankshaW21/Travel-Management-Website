Gugamal National Park

Gugamal National Park is a delightful wildlife reserve which will offer you lots of opportunities to sight a variety of distinct animals and birds such as tigers, crocodiles, bisons, gawas, nilgais and wild deer.


Also, the park takes care of a variety of flora and fauna and is famous for its distinct culture. One can take part in numerous fun filled activities here such as trekking, photo clicking, bird watching sessions and safari rides.






Location: Gugamal national Park, Amravati District, Maharashtra


Timings: 6 AM to 3 PM


Entry fee:INR 2000


Best time to visit: October to June


Nearby attractions:Amravati, Omkareshwar
