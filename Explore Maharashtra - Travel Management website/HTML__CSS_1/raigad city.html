Raigad's Architectural Marvels

Raigad is a district located in the state of Maharashtra, India. It is situated in the Konkan region of the state and is known for its historical and scenic significance. Here is some information about Raigad district:

Raigad district is located in the western part of Maharashtra and is bordered by the Arabian Sea to the west. It has a diverse landscape that includes coastal areas, hills, and forests.

The district is historically important as it was the capital of the Maratha Empire under the rule of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. The Raigad Fort, perched on a hill, was the capital of the Maratha Empire and is a major historical attraction in the region.

The economy of Raigad district is primarily based on agriculture, fishing, and tourism. The region's proximity to Mumbai, the financial capital of India, has also contributed to its economic development.
