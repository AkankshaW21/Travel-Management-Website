Mumbai's Architectural Marvels

Mumbai is not a district in itself but the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra. Mumbai is a vast and densely populated metropolis located on the west coast of India. However, it is part of the larger Mumbai Metropolitan Region (MMR), which includes several districts and cities in the surrounding area. Here is some information about Mumbai and the districts within the Mumbai Metropolitan Region:

Mumbai City is one of the districts within the Mumbai Metropolitan Region. It is the administrative district that covers the southern part of Mumbai, including areas like Colaba, Malabar Hill, and Marine Lines. It is the financial, commercial, and entertainment hub of India.

Mumbai Suburban is another district within the Mumbai Metropolitan Region. It encompasses the northern and western suburbs of Mumbai, including areas like Bandra, Andheri, and Borivali. This district is densely populated and includes many residential and commercial areas.
