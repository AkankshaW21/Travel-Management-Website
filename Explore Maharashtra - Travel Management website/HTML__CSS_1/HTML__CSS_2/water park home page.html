Maharashtra Aqua Adventure Water Park


Maharashtra, a state in western India, offers several water parks for residents and tourists to enjoy a fun-filled day in the water.




Imagica Water Park

Experiencing a full day of fun and frolic at one of the best Maharashtra water parks will be the real revelation. Dunk yourself into fun as you experience various rides from family rides to couple rides and more.

From loopy slides to open speed slides, from swirling rides to the ones that Boomerang you down to up Imagica has rides that go beyond your imagination. Apart from the unique selection of rides, it also has various restaurants that offer food across many different cuisines to fulfil those taste buds after a rejuvenating water experience.


Entry Fee: Adults - Rs.899, Children - Rs.799


Timings: 10:30 am - & 7:00 pm


Location:30/31, Khopoli-Pali Road, SH 92, Taluka Khalapur, District Raigad, Sangadewadi, Maharashtra

Shangrila Resort & Waterpark

With spectacular sights and activities galore, anything less than two nights spent here would be a travesty. A popular destination for a day spend among the locals, this waterpark is really pretty apart from being clean and adventurous.

A complete family waterpark, each ride is customized keeping in mind shapes, sizes and age of all the members in a family. Hold hands while you all zip through the family slide, or simply bust some dance moves under the waterfall. Splash around in the splash pool, or simply lay low as your tube takes you places through the lazy river.


Entry Fee:Adults- Rs.700 onwards, Child - Rs.650 onwards


Timings: 10:00 am - 6:00 pm


Location: Mumbai-Nashik Highway, Bhiwandi ByPass End, Gangaram Pada, Vadpe, Bhiwandi, Thane, Maharashtra

Krazy Castle Aqua Park

With a number of fun rides, Krazy Castle Aqua Park, like any other amusement and water park has attracted a number of visitors of years now. It counts as one of the popular places to visit in Nagpur and sees a footfall of people from nearby cities as well.

The park keeps introducing attractive packages for groups, exclusive offers for corporate offices, school and college students, etc. For a fun-filled day, one can opt to visit this park - best during hot and humid summer days of Nagpur city.


Location:Ambazari Road.


Timings:11 AM to 6 PM


Entry fee: INR 280 onwards.

Water Kingdom

Spend your summer Sundays splashing about in the water, as you relax in one of the most stunning water parks of India. With a speed of up to 40 miles per hour, the adult slide or roller coaster is one of the most daring rides of this waterpark.

Being Asia's largest theme park, it also offers rain showers, dance floor with laser lights, splash roof, water guns and a wave pool. The lagoon is a fun filled package with 10 slides spread across 3 kiddies slides, 4 family slides and 3 adult thrill slides. It also ensures you are drenched with a splash from the 500 liter elephant bucket every time you slide down.


Location: Global Pagoda Road, Essel world Amusement Park, Borivali West, Mumbai, Maharashtra


Timings:10:00 am - & 7:00 pm


Entry fee:Adults - Rs.1310, Children - Rs.865, Senior Citizen - Rs.865

Dreamworld Fun & Water

A weekend spent splashing and diving in the water activities at one of the best ones among Maharashtra water parks will surely be an exhilarating experience. Experience the true spirit of summer as you dash and dive in the wave pools, glide through various water slides and enjoy it with both family and friends.

The most popular slide is the family raft slide among other slides such as body slides and tube slides. This place is mostly occupied owing to the latest sky magic vortex park and the rain dance preferred by big groups.


Location: Kasaba Bawada Road, Raman Mala, Kolhapur, Maharashtra


Timings:10: 00 am - 9:00 pm


Entry fee:Adults - Rs.550 onwards, Kids Rs.430 onwards depending on the time slot you choose

Tikuji Ni Wadi

A trip to one of the least popular water parks in Maharashtra will help you focus on the present instead of constantly thinking about the past or future. With a special water play area for kids; this place is surely going to have you thrilled by the first sight of it.

Located between Mumbai and Thane, this place is going to have you spend a few hours being a sloth in the lazy river. It also features giant slides, wave pool, family slides, kiddie slides, and aqua play shop and landscaped gardens to make you have a fulfilling experience.


Location:Thane Road, Manpada Village, Chitalsar, Thane, Maharashtra


Timings:10:00 am - 6:00 pm


Entry fee: Rs.750 onwards, depending on the package you choose

wet n joy water park

Bring the focus back to yourself as you get wet and wild at one of the most stunning water parks in Maharashtra. With kid-friendly and amusing characters built in this area, the “Fungama” definitely is a favourite play area for toddlers and kids in this water park.

Put yourself on a tube, and let the moving river drag you from one end to the other without you having to make an effort. Race with your buddies on a mat, a few of you are put together on parallel slides and it's all about who drowns first. Covering an area of more than 10,000 square feet, this waterpark is home to India's biggest Aqua play station.


Location:Old Pune Mumbai Highway, NH-4 Post-Takve, Mundhaware, Maharashtra


Timings:10: 00 am - 6:00 pm


Entry fee:Adults - Rs.899 onwards, Child - Rs.799 onwards depending on which part of the year you visit

suraj water park

Whether you know how to swim or just like to languish around like the little mermaid, the Suraj Water Park has more than you can desire. With a terrific location among lush green valleys, you've missed something in this lifetime if you haven;t visited this water park yet.

Holding the title of “The Pride of Maharashtra”, this water park has 6 records in the Limca Book of Records. With the largest man-made fibre cave 103 ft. high, this one;s a visual treat beyond relief from the heat. With Hindi names of rides and a statue of Lord Shiva, this one's a favourite among the desis.


Location:Ghodbunder Rd, Dongripada, Thane West, Thane, Maharashtra


Timings: 10:00 am - 6:00 pm


Entry fee:Adults: Rs.800, Child: according to height -From 3'5" to 4'5" feet Rs.650; below 3'5" feet FREE entry

Sentosa Water Park

Sentosa Water Park is a popular one owing to a lot of water activities it offers for the non-swimmers as well. One of the best waterparks in Pune, this one's popular in the masses owing to the affordable entry ticket yet offering everyone fun rides with the latest technology.

The most desired activities include the high-intensity waterfall, the wave pool, the tube twister slides, the water play system among other common ones. One can also spend the weekend at the resort of which the water park is a part. Also, snacks and food can be served around the poolside to keep your taste buds tantalizing while you enjoy a dip.


Location: Mumbai-Pune Expressway, 1 Km Before Ravet, Ravet, Pune, Maharashtra


Timings:10:00 am - 6: 00 pm


Entry fee: Rs.350 onwards

Krushnai Water Park

Squeeze in a quick day trip to one of the best water parks in Maharashtra. Also known as the Krushnai Water world, this water park has one of the best locations in India. Famous for the numerous slides it offers, this waterpark is located among the gorgeous green hills near Sinhagad fort.

An Aqua trail, extreme turnpikes, twisters, a pirate island, black holes and a wave pool are some of its major attractions. If you're in for an adrenaline rush, this place is for you. Spend an entire day, and you'll still go back wanting for more.


Location:Adults - Rs.500 onwards, Kids - Rs.400 onwards


Timings: 10:00 am - 6:00 pm


Entry fee: Base, Gat No. 186, Donje Gaon-Sinhagad Road, Sinhgad Rd, Donje Gaon, Maharashtra
