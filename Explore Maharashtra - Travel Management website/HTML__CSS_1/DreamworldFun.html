Dreamworld Fun & Water Park

This water park is located in the heart of Kolhapur and offers a wide range of thrilling attractions and exciting activities that will leave you with unforgettable memories.


Its impressive selection of water slides is one of its highlights, ranging from heart-pumping plunges to gentle rides perfect for families. With something for everyone, whether you're looking for a wild adventure or a relaxing float down the lazy river, this water park has got you covered.






Location: Kasaba Bawada Road, Raman Mala, Kolhapur, Maharashtra


Timings:10: 00 am - 9:00 pm


Entry fee:Adults - Rs.550 onwards, Kids Rs.430 onwards depending on the time slot you choose

Another standout feature of the park is its massive wave pool. Here, you can experience the thrill of swimming in an ocean as waves crash around you. It's the perfect spot to cool off and have fun with your loved ones.
For those seeking even more excitement, the park offers a variety of water-based activities, such as rain dance parties and water volleyball. These interactive experiences add extra fun to your visit, ensuring that your trip to Dream World Water Park is full of laughter and adventure from beginning to end.


And when it comes to the practicalities of your visit, Dream World Water Park has got you covered. The facilities, including the clean changing rooms and ample seating areas, are well-maintained, and the park's friendly staff are always on hand to ensure your safety and provide assistance whenever needed.


Dream World Water Park Kolhapur is a thrilling and exciting destination for water enthusiasts of all ages. Located in Kolhapur, Maharashtra, this water park offers attractions and activities to keep visitors cool during the hot summer months and entertained. The park boasts an array of exhilarating water slides. Visitors can also enjoy relaxing in the lazy river or taking a dip in the wave pool, which simulates the feeling of swimming in the ocean.
