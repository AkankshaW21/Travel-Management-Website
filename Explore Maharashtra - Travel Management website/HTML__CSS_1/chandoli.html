Chandoli National Park

Chandoli National Park is considered to be one of the most charming national parks in Maharashtra for wildlife and nature lovers. This park is a breeding ground of a variety of animals that includes the powerful Bengal tiger and also, sambhar, deer. You will be surrounded by utmost greenery and lush atmosphere. Notably, this park shelters a lot of distinct birds and animals. If you are a die hard adventure lover, then you must give a visit this national park.


You can take part in numerous thrilling activities such as birdwatching sessions, boating, safari rides, trekking and much more. On top of that, you will get to discover quite a lot of nearby top attractions such as Kandhar Doh and Kandhar Doh Falls, Tenali Falls, Chandoli Dam, Vasant Nagar Reservoir and Kokan Darshan.






Location:Satara District, Kolhapur District, Sangli District, Maharashtra


Timings: 7 AM to 10 AM and 3 PM to 6 PM


Entry fee:INR 300


Best time to visit: October to February.


Nearby attractions:Amba Ghat, Shri Amba Bai, Temple, Kolhapur, Rankala Lake
