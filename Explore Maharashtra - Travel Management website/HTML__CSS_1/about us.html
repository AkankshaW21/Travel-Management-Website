About Us

Who Are We

We are students of Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune. From the IT department, C section, group 15.

At Explore Maharashtra, we believe that travel goes beyond the destinations; it's about the people you meet, the cultures you discover, and the stories you create. Our mission is to curate exceptional travel experiences that cater to your unique interests and desires.
At Maharashtra Explorer, we're on a mission to connect travelers and explorers with the best of what Maharashtra has to offer.
We've meticulously categorized and curated tourist places, whether historical, religious, natural, or culinary, to cater to your unique interests and preferences.
Your experiences matter to us. We believe that authentic traveler reviews are invaluable for fellow explorers seeking memorable journeys.
Feel free to share your thoughts, insights, and tips after visiting these incredible destinations.
Your reviews not only help others but also contribute to the ever-growing treasure trove of Maharashtra's tourist experiences.

Our Mission

To be your trusted travel companion in discovering the breathtaking beauty and rich cultural heritage of Maharashtra. We're dedicated to curating and categorizing the most captivating tourist places, from historical landmarks to spiritual sanctuaries and everything in between. Our goal is to provide you with a seamless and memorable journey, whether you're a history buff, a spiritual seeker, a nature enthusiast, or a culinary explorer.
