Tadoba National Park

The major attraction of the Tadoba national park is a wildlife safari. The park was declared a Project Tiger reserve in the year 1993. Besides the tigers, you will also get to witness various other wild species like leopards, gaur, wild dogs, sloth bears, jungle cats, hyenas, sambar, cheetah, nilgai and barking deer while on the safari.


The park is also a dwelling to about 181 species of birds which include endangered species and a lot of water birds. You will also get to witness a lot of swampy crocodiles and migratory ducks around the Tadoba Lake. The Tadoba Lake is located at the center of the Tadoba-Andhari tiger reserve; one can find swampy crocodile and birds such as changeable hawk eagle, black-hooded oriole, monarch flycatcher, grey-hornbill and golden-oriole.
