Shangrila Resort & Waterpark

Magic of Joy Beyond your imagination Shangrila waterpark

The perfect retreat is only a pleasurable one hour drive from the suburbs of Mumbai, like Dadar, Bandra. Eleven Kilometers from Bhiwandi-Kalyan junction on the Mumbai - Nashik Highway. It is a spectacular place for those who yearn for moments of pure enjoyment with family and friends every now and then. Set amidst a crescent of hills under a vast uninterrupted sky, Shangrila Resort is ready to welcome you for the great splash of fun.


At Shangrila Resort the craziest, funkiest way to cool off is of course at the Water Park spread over 15 acres of land. With the most modern and exciting water games, your entire family is sure to turn into a pack of wild kids! Isn't that what you desire once in a while? And what you deserve. Check the temptations here! Go down the Family Slide hand in hand in an outrageous din or hold your breath as you slide along the loops of the Water Slide at breakneck speed and splash gloriously into the pool. Take on the teasing sprays of the Jacuzzi or just waddle around in bright little floating tubes. Then there are smaller water slides for the more cautious ones too!






Entry Fee:Adults- Rs.700 onwards, Child - Rs.650 onwards


Timings: 10:00 am - 6:00 pm


Location: Mumbai-Nashik Highway, Bhiwandi ByPass End, Gangaram Pada, Vadpe, Bhiwandi, Thane, Maharashtra


The swimming pool invites the young and old for a leisurely dip into its safe, user-friendly depth of 41/2 feet. So while even toddlers can take their first swimming lessons here under the keen eye of Mom and Dad, Grandma and Grandpa can also enjoy a safe splash or two here. To put it simply, once you are here you would want to spend hours together-splashing about, swimming, spraying water, and just enjoying the magic of water. Besides, the top quality maintenance of the place and continuous supply of water from the filtration plant ensure complete safety and hygiene for our visitors. All this at a very moderate entrance fee. The high point of Shangrila Resort, this is a place where you can really wash away the stress of the city routine and revive the mind and body to incredible measures. By the way you would know that baths are scientifically proven to have a therapeutic effect on body and mind.


You are pleased to enjoy The Great Ever-Flowing Waterfall to your family. A completely safe and totally hygienic waterfall. It flows continuous all the year around rejuvenates you and you can feel the ardently flowing now. The Great Refresher, which is undoubtedly the high point of this gorgeous Resort, is a First time facility provided in Region, along with many splashes.


Add to it the thrill of monsoon, and you will find yourself drenched all over in pure delight. Literally and figuratively! Just think of it! The overcast sky, the drizzle turning into a heavy downpour, the shimmering pool and the shivering you! That's the point to get closer to life, to Nature, to its sensuality. At Shangrila Resort, we keep this magic of Life alive in your heart.
